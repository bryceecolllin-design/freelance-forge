About Freelance-Forge

Freelance-Forge (freelance-forge.com) is a marketplace where customers connect with independent professionals across general contracting, fabrication, design, and more.

  • Post a project with your requirements and (optionally) files or drawings.
  • Contractors browse and place bids — you can message freely before awarding.
  • Pick the best fit by reviewing profiles, ratings, and communication.

Who pays what

Customers can post projects and browse contractors at no charge. Contractors pay a subscription to submit bids on open jobs (processed securely via Stripe). Direct payment for the work itself is between you and your client—this site does not hold project funds.

Staying Safe

Always review profiles, ratings, and message thoroughly before awarding. For larger jobs, consider written agreements and staged milestones.
