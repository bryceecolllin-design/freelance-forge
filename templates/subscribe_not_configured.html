Subscriptions not configured

Contractor billing runs through Stripe. Set these environment variables on the server, create a recurring Price in the Stripe Dashboard, then restart the app: 

SECRET_KEY=your-long-random-string
STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=sk_live_... or sk_test_...
STRIPE_PRICE_ID=price_...
STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET=whsec_...
STRIPE_TRIAL_DAYS=15

In Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Customer portal, turn on the billing portal so contractors can cancel or update cards. Add a webhook endpoint for /stripe/webhook (use your public URL, e.g. https://yourdomain.com/stripe/webhook) and listen for checkout.session.completed, customer.subscription.updated, and customer.subscription.deleted.

Until then, if Stripe is disabled, bidding may be allowed without a subscription (development only).
